This graph shows the number of dwellings for which building consents have been granted, as well as estimated population increase. This is meant to provide a rough indication of over-supply and under-supply of housing over time.
Due to the population data available, this graph uses years from July-June rather than calendar years. As a result, values may seem different to those on other graphs on this website.
Consented housing capacity is estimated by multiplying the number of consented dwellings by the average Wellington City household size of 2.6 (as at the 2013 Census). In addition to population growth, extra demand for dwellings will also arise from changes of household formation (e.g. the trend for smaller households).
